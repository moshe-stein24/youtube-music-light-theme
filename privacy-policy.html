Privacy Policy for YouTube Music Light Theme

Last updated: June 8, 2025

Overview

This extension applies a light theme to YouTube Music to improve your browsing experience. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

This extension:

  • Does not collect any personal data
  • Does not track your browsing activity
  • Does not store any information about the websites you visit
  • Does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies
  • Does not communicate with external servers
  • Only works on music.youtube.com

How the Extension Works

The extension operates entirely locally on your device by:

  • Modifying the visual appearance of YouTube Music (applying light theme)
  • Storing your theme preferences locally in your browser
  • Reading YouTube Music page content only to apply styling changes
  • Running only when you visit music.youtube.com

All data remains on your device and is never transmitted elsewhere.

Data Storage

Any settings or preferences are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's built-in storage API. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is only accessible by this extension
  • Can be cleared by uninstalling the extension
  • Only relates to your theme preferences

Permissions Explained

This extension requests only the "activeTab" permission, which allows it to:

  • Access the current YouTube Music tab when you click the extension icon
  • Apply styling changes to improve the visual appearance
  • This permission does NOT allow access to other tabs or websites

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at this URL with an updated "Last updated" date.
